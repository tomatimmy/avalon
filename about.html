Avalon Initiative

At Avalon, we’ve embarked on a mission to breathe new life into a former pasture, turning it into a thriving hub for regenerative living and learning. We believe in the power of diversity, where people from various backgrounds come together to inspire and support one another in creating a better world.

Alex’s Story

My life resembles a colorful tapestry, woven with an array of experiences and interests. I am a refugee child turned university professor, a social media and community researcher turned permaculture practitioner, a mother, a grandmother, an adventurer with an insatiable thirst for freedom, and a devoted family person as well. My heart beats to the rhythm of bustling cities, yet finds solace in the tranquility and nurturing embrace of nature.

A Playground for Change

Avalon is where my journey finds its focal point, where dreams find their expression, and where my heart finds its home. It's our adventure playground, a space where I can authentically be myself, living in alignment with my core values and beliefs. Here, we cultivate not just gardens but a way of life. We nurture the soil and our souls.

My multicultural background has given me a unique perspective on the diverse cultures in this region. It's not just about recognizing diversity; it's about nurturing it. Diversity, whether in ecosystems or communities, is essential for resilience and vitality.

Teaching Through Experience

As a university professor, my mission has always been to make knowledge accessible and fun. Permaculture, with its transformative potential, should be no exception. My experience in the classroom has taught me that education is a two-way street, a dynamic exchange that creates new perspectives on realities. At Avalon, we're not just teaching permaculture; we're living it.

My life's story may be a kaleidoscope of experiences, but at its core, it's a story of transformation, connection, and growth. And now, as I embark on this permaculture adventure with you, I invite you to become a part of this narrative, to explore, learn, and create a reality worth living.

Team

We come from diverse cultural backgrounds, each with unique skills and experiences. But we are united by a shared vision: to create a sanctuary that sparks inspiration and empowerment, allowing individuals to embrace a life of freedom and harmonious coexistence with each other and nature.

GABRIEL

Costa Rican by birth, Swiss by current residence, Gabriel's canvas is life itself. A realist with an adventurer's spirit, he's constantly seeking alternative life models. For him, life is art, and he's the artist.

ALEX

A fusion of Polish and Swiss roots, Alex's journey has taken her from the lecture halls of a university professor to the vibrant world of permaculture teaching. A wanderer between cultures, she’s not just a dreamer of change; she's a living embodiment of it.

OSCAR

A true Tico, Oscar isn't just an architect; he's a draftsman of dreams, a designer of possibilities. Always on the lookout for new challenges, his mission is to unearth the best in humanity through thoughtful design.

JUAN

Among us, Juan is the local sage. He grew up here, a repository of knowledge and experience. Avalon's trusted advisor, he's the bridge to indigenous wisdom, an invaluable source of insight.
