Courses

The curriculum of our permaculture education program is buzzing with vibrant energy, ready to flourish soon.

You can shape our program with your ideas, aspirations, and dreams. Get in touch if you have specific learning interests or knowledge to share.

GET IN TOUCH

Immersive Permaculture Adventure at Avalon

Do you need a fresh perspective? Join our immersive permaculture experiences in the lush landscapes of Costa Rica. Whether you choose to stay at Avalon itself or in the indigenous territory, you’re guaranteed an authentic and transformative adventure.

Cultivating Young Eco-Leaders of Tomorrow

In close partnership with local schools, we’re offering courses specially designed for young minds and future eco-leaders. We aim to kindle a passion for regenerative and eco-friendly practices within the local community, inspiring intercultural exchange, fostering a wholesome lifestyle, and nurturing food sovereignty. If you’re enthusiastic about becoming a volunteer teacher, your open heart and mind are always welcome.

Self-Study: Your Personal Permaculture Expedition

Our self-study courses offer the possibility of learning on your own terms. Study with us anytime, anywhere. You’re in control, deciding when to start and when to complete your journey. But there’s more; our courses are designed to pull you away from screens and into the embrace of nature. Dive into workbooks, absorb enlightening recordings, and take on challenges in the great outdoors, whether it’s your backyard or a local park

Teaming Up for Real-World Impact

Do you spot a pressing issue that, if solved, could profoundly improve the well- being of people, animals, or the environment? If you lack the necessary skills to tackle it alone, we’ve got you covered. We’ll connect you with Avalon’s global and multidisciplinary network and provide you with the tools and coaching required for your success. Together, we can turn your vision into a transformative reality.

A Sneak Peek of Course Contents

Introduction to Tropical Permaculture: Discover the foundations of permaculture with a focus on indigenous knowledge.

Community Building: Find your tribe, both online and in the real world, to support your permaculture journey.

Crafting Regenerative Livelihoods: Learn how to earn a living while aligning with permaculture principles and regenerative practices.

Harnessing the Power of Plants: Explore the incredible potential of plants for health, well-being, and sustainable living.

Food Sovereignty: Master the art of cultivating your food even in limited spaces, promoting self-sufficiency.

Ecosocial Design: Dive into the principles of reimagining our systems to create a more sustainable and harmonious world.
