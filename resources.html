Resources

Permaculture is not about perfection or achieving some kind of ideal. It’s about taking small steps towards a more sustainable and regenerative way of life. By applying permaculture principles in our daily lives, we can make a positive impact on ourselves, our communities, and the planet.

Our downloadable manuals offer practical tips for integrating permaculture into your life, whether you have a garden, a backyard, or even just a cozy balcony. Don’t have outdoor space? No worries, we’ve got suggestions for you too.

Explore a web of connections through our carefully curated links. These lead you to people and organizations that have played pivotal roles in our permaculture journey.

Step into our world through our inspiring blog posts. Journey alongside us in Costa Rica as we explore topics like tropical permaculture, regenerative living, ecosocial design, and the wisdom of plants.
