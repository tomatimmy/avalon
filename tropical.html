Tropical Permaculture

Permaculture is a way of life that seeks to create a sustainable and regenerative world for future generations.Tropical permaculture builds on the knowledge and practices of indigenous communities, who have been living in harmony with nature for centuries. It incorporates traditional knowledge and practices of indigenous communities into permaculture

Nestled in the heart of Costa Rica’s tropics, Avalon’s permaculture is guided by the rich biodiversity of tropical ecosystems. Our year-round growing season empowers us to cultivate a diverse range of crops, creating a vibrant, sustainable tapestry of food systems.

We collaborate closely with local communities and indigenous peoples, integrating their deep connection to the land into Avalon’s practices. By doing so, we cultivate a permaculture that honours both, cultural sensitivity and ecological regeneration

Rooted in Ethical Foundations

Permaculture principles are based on observing and imitating natural ecosystems, which produce no waste and are self-sufficient. They apply not only to gardening or sustainable living but also to social justice and equity. Imagine permaculture as a holistic journey where you’re in the driver’s seat, making choices that not only bring you joy but also benefit others and nurture our planet.

At Avalon, we’re deeply rooted in the ethical foundations of permaculture: People Care, Earth Care, and Fair Share. These principles are more than just words; they’re the very essence of our values, driving our unwavering commitment to creating a world that cherishes its people, its environment, and its future.

People Care

Humans are integral to every ecosystem, wielding the power to shape it for better or worse. At Avalon, we hold the well-being of every individual engaged in our permaculture journey in the highest regard. We firmly believe that working in harmony with nature should be an experience filled with joy and anticipation. When you step onto our property, you become a part of this nurturing ecosystem. We're dedicated to your health and vitality, providing nourishing and medicinal plants, and tranquil spaces for reflection, meditation, or simply connecting with nature. Here, your time isn't just productive; it's profoundly rejuvenating.

Earth Care

Our commitment to Earth Care runs deep, encompassing our responsibility to safeguard a healthy environment for today and generations yet to come. Avalon is on a mission to restore a once-degraded pasture into a thriving, biodiverse ecosystem. It's a journey of healing, for both the land and our souls. Each day, we labor to reduce our ecological footprint, revitalize soil health, and foster biodiversity through native plantings and wildlife havens. Our goal is to transform this land into a haven of abundance and resilience, a sanctuary for all inhabitants, from the tiniest insects to the towering trees. We share our experiences and insights openly because we believe in the immense power of small actions, and we thrive on inspiring and being inspired.

Fair share

The principle of Fair Share reminds us to strive for the equitable distribution of Avalon's abundance among all living beings. Surplus produce is generously shared, not only among people but also with the birds and other animals who call our land home. However, our most heartfelt commitment lies in our close collaboration with the local community. We stand with them, supporting them in shaping livelihoods that honor their heritage and incorporate the wisdom of their ancestors. This support contributes to securing their rightful place in a region with a history of neglect and exploitation. Our objective is to reduce reliance on exploitative structures and foster a secure foundation from which to challenge injustice, ultimately inspiring others to do the same.
